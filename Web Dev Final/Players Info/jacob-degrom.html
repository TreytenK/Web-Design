Jacob deGrom

The Dominant Ace

Early Life

Jacob deGrom was born on June 19, 1988, in DeLand, Florida. He played baseball as a shortstop in high school and transitioned to pitching during his college years at Stetson University.

Professional Career

Jacob deGrom was drafted by the New York Mets in the ninth round of the 2010 MLB Draft. He made his MLB debut in 2014 and quickly established himself as one of the best pitchers in baseball.

DeGrom is known for his electric fastball, pinpoint command, and calm demeanor on the mound. He currently plays for the Texas Rangers, bringing his elite skills to their rotation.

Achievements

  • 2-time NL Cy Young Award winner (2018, 2019)
  • MLB All-Star (multiple years)
  • Led the league in ERA multiple times
  • Known for his historic streak of quality starts

Personal Life

Off the field, Jacob deGrom is known for his humble personality and strong family values. He enjoys spending time with his wife and children, and he remains a role model for young athletes.
