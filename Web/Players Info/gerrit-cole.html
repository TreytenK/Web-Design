Gerrit Cole

The Power Pitcher

Early Life

Gerrit Cole was born on September 8, 1990, in Newport Beach, California. Growing up in a baseball-loving family, he excelled on the mound from a young age, setting the foundation for his professional career.

Professional Career

Cole was drafted first overall in the 2011 MLB Draft by the Pittsburgh Pirates. He made his MLB debut in 2013 and quickly became one of the most dominant pitchers in the league. After stints with the Pirates and Houston Astros, Cole signed a record-breaking contract with the New York Yankees in 2019.

Known for his blazing fastball and sharp breaking pitches, Cole has been a consistent Cy Young Award contender and a leader in the Yankees' rotation.

Achievements

  • MLB All-Star (multiple years)
  • AL ERA leader (2019)
  • Record-breaking strikeout performances in postseason play
  • Consistent Cy Young Award finalist

Personal Life

Off the field, Gerrit Cole is known for his competitive spirit and professionalism. He is married to Amy Crawford, and the couple is active in charitable causes, often giving back to their community.
