Aaron Judge

The Towering Slugger

Early Life

Aaron Judge was born on April 26, 1992, in Linden, California. Adopted shortly after birth, he grew up in a supportive family that encouraged his athletic pursuits. Judge excelled in multiple sports before focusing on baseball.

Professional Career

Judge was drafted by the New York Yankees in the first round of the 2013 MLB Draft. He made his MLB debut in 2016 and quickly established himself as a premier power hitter, smashing a record 52 home runs as a rookie in 2017.

Since then, Judge has become the face of the Yankees, renowned for his prodigious home runs, excellent defensive skills, and leadership both on and off the field.

Achievements

  • AL Rookie of the Year (2017)
  • 4-time MLB All-Star
  • AL MVP (2022)
  • Set the single-season AL home run record with 62 home runs in 2022

Personal Life

Aaron Judge is known for his humility and commitment to community service. He actively supports various charitable organizations and inspires young athletes through his work ethic and sportsmanship.
