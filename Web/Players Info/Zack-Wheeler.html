Zack Wheeler

An Ace on the Mound

Early Life

Zack Wheeler was born on May 30, 1990, in Smyrna, Georgia. Growing up, he developed a passion for baseball and honed his skills as a pitcher, becoming a standout player in high school.

Professional Career

Wheeler was drafted sixth overall in the 2009 MLB Draft by the San Francisco Giants. He made his MLB debut in 2013 with the New York Mets and quickly established himself as a reliable starting pitcher.

In 2020, Wheeler signed with the Philadelphia Phillies, where he has become an integral part of their pitching rotation, earning recognition for his durability, velocity, and command on the mound.

Achievements

  • MLB All-Star (2021)
  • Led MLB in complete games and shutouts in 2021
  • Runner-up for the NL Cy Young Award (2021)

Personal Life

Off the field, Zack Wheeler is known for his strong work ethic and humble personality. He enjoys spending time with his family and giving back to the community through various charitable efforts.
